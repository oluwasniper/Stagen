01  Who We Are

Scagen is a QR-code scanning and generation app. Any questions about this policy can be sent to christian@sonofnos.com.

02  What We Collect

Category Data Destination
Account Email & password hash (registered users) or anonymous session ID (guests) Appwrite cloud (EU)
QR history QR codes you scan or generate, linked to your account ID Appwrite cloud (EU)
Analytics opt-in App events (screen views, taps, errors), device type, app version. Identified by opaque UUID — never name or email. PostHog (US)
Push token FCM/APNs device token for push notifications Appwrite + Firebase
Preferences Vibration, beep, language, analytics toggle — encrypted on-device only Your device only

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, or payment information. Contact data imported for QR generation is processed on-device and never stored server-side.

03  Why We Collect It

Contract performance
To create and manage your account and sync your QR history across devices.
Legitimate interests
To maintain app stability, diagnose crashes, and improve performance.
Consent — analytics
Analytics are off by default. Enable them in Settings → Privacy → Analytics. Withdraw consent any time — your identity is reset immediately.
Consent — push notifications
You are prompted for permission. Revoke it any time in your device settings.

04  Analytics in Detail

When opted in, we use PostHog to collect anonymised usage events — which screens you visit, which QR types you generate, and performance metrics. We never pass your email or name to PostHog, only an opaque Appwrite user UUID.

On sign-out your PostHog identity is reset so sessions are not linked together. Toggle analytics any time via Settings → Privacy → Analytics.

05  Data Retention

Account & QR history
Retained while your account is active. Deleting your account removes all data from Appwrite within 30 days.
Analytics events
Retained in PostHog for 1 year, then automatically deleted.
Push tokens
Removed from Appwrite when you sign out or uninstall the app.

08  Children

Scagen is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/EEA). We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us if you believe a child has provided personal data and we will delete it promptly.

09  Security

Data in transit is encrypted via TLS. Data at rest in Appwrite is encrypted at the storage level. App preferences are stored using platform encrypted storage (Android Keystore / iOS Secure Enclave). We follow responsible disclosure practices.

10  Changes to This Policy

When we update this policy we will update the date above. For material changes we will notify you via an in-app notification. Continued use after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.
