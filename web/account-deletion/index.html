Account Deletion

Delete your
account & data.

You can delete your account in-app or by submitting the form below.

Option 1  — In the App

1
Open Settings
Tap the settings icon from the bottom navigation bar.
2
Go to Account
Tap Account → Delete Account.
3
Confirm deletion
Confirm the prompt. Your session ends immediately and all data is queued for removal.

Option 2  — Submit a Request

No longer have access to the app? Fill in the form below and we'll process your request within 30 days.

Request received

We'll process your deletion request within 30 days and confirm by email.

What Gets Deleted

Your account
Email address, password hash, and session data — permanently removed.
QR history
All scanned and generated QR codes synced to your account.
Push tokens & notifications
Device push tokens and stored in-app notifications.
Analytics identity
Your PostHog UUID is reset. Aggregated events (no PII) may be retained up to 1 year per our retention policy.

Heads up: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Local data is cleared when you uninstall the app.
